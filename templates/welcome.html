Welcome {{ username }}

This Website lets you upload your favorite videos. You can also view other users videos and vote on them

If a video gets a vote score of -5 then the video gets deleted from the website so keep that in mind and only post quality content!

You can also repost(save) a video of another user to your My Videos page and this will have a status of reposted. Reposted videos can't be viewed or voted on by other users and are only viewable on the My Videos sections

The My Videos section is only visible once you save a video to your profile or unless you create a new video. These videos can be ordered via category, reposted(saved) videos and original videos posted by the user

The All Videos section contains only original posted content by other users and no reposts and is subjected to the voting process and these videos can be ordered by category and user

Any original videos that you have posted are only viewable in the All Videos sections to other users, but are still viewable in your own My Videos section. So don't worry your original videos are viewable to other users :)
